Care & accommodation options to suit you

Matamata Country Lodge offers a total care concept with a wide range of accommodation available, from 28 two bedroom Chalets (8 brand new), Assisted Living Apartments, Rest Home or Hospital Level Care, Day Care and Respite. Find out more about each below.

2-bedroom chalets - now available.

Two bedroom chalets

With the addition of eight brand new 2-bedroom chalets (more below), we now have a total of 28 chalets - offering a superb independent living option. All chalets feature full kitchens and bathrooms and the grounds are expertly managed by our staff gardeners. For additional piece of mind, all chalets feature emergency alarms.

New 2-Bed chalets

We have a range of new 2-bedroom chalets (a small number of chalets are still available for sale). If you would like to inquire about one of our new chalets please contact us.

Assisted living apartments

When some additional care is required, our assisted living apartments are an excellent option. We have various levels of care available to suit the individual needs of our assisted living residents - with a focus on maximising the residents privacy and independence.

Access to care options

We offer our residents a full Diversional Therapy and activity programme, rehabilitation services and regular on-site specialist visits.

Rest home and Hospital level care

We provide rest home and full hospital level care in our purpose built facilities. It's our ongoing goal to provide the highest level of care and respect to our residents. We have many friendly, highly qualified, and dedicated staff members across the facility in order to provide the utmost care for our residents.

Book a visit / contact us

Day care and respite

We often invite people to come and spend the week days with us and we offer a pick-up and drop-off service. A full activity programme is on offer along with lovely home cooked meals (with meat sourced from a local butchery and fruit and vege from a local supplier). We also offer long and short term Respite Care and you may quality for a subsidy (contact us to discuss).
